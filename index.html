Game Rules 🎮

  1. Player 1 get his turn before Player 2 🔁
  2. After each person's turn, a round is completed 🤝
  3. During a person's turn, either the roll die button or the double or nothing (DoN) button can be used 🔀
  4. The roll dice button adds a random number between 1 and 6 to your score 🎲
  5. The DoN button appears only after the 1st round 🎰
  6. The DoN button either doubles your score or leaves it the same 😱
  7. The WINNER is the player with a score HIGHER THAN OR EQUAL TO 20 after a round is completed 🏆
